THE IDEA

Design and manufacture a pendant light fixture for the average family income to afford. The light must be an easy assembles, that does not require any sort of bonding agent such as glue. As we are becoming more and more waste full the light fixture must be simple enough to sit in a flat pack box. Flat packaging will also reduce the amount of space required for storage in a warehouse. The packaging should need to be compact enough and strong to hold not just the parts but protect the bulb and electrical plug without any breakages. As the light fixture is aimed that average income families; the box must be as light as possible will as little wasted space.
